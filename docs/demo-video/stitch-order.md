# Demo Video Stitch Order

These clips are designed to be stitched around the live walkthrough.

1. `intro.mp4` - 8 seconds
2. Your live walkthrough - call, SMS link, Gemini understanding, tapback approval, Notion save
3. `tech-explainer.mp4` - 14 seconds
4. Optional final live proof shot - saved Notion page or final Sendblue message

Audio recommendation:

- Use voiceover over `intro.mp4`, `tech-explainer.mp4`, and `outro.mp4`.

- Duck or mute live audio during SMS, tapback, and Notion shots if you narrate over them.
