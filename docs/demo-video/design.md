# Demo Video Design

## Visual Identity

Dark premium console editorial. The video should feel like a composed technical artifact for applied AI builders: sharp, cinematic, precise, and slightly dramatic without becoming cyberpunk.

## Palette

- Background: `#080B10`
- Deep panel: `#10141C`
- Elevated panel: `#171C26`
- Foreground: `#F4F1EA`
- Muted text: `#B9C0CC`
- Quiet text: `#7E8796`
- Line: `#2B3343`
- Red-orange accent: `#FF4D2E`
- Console green: `#35D08A`
- Approval amber: `#F7B731`

## Typography

- Display: `Space Grotesk`, weights `800` and `900`
- Body: `Space Grotesk`, weights `350` and `500`
- Technical: `IBM Plex Mono`, weights `600` and `700`

## Motion

- Snappy editorial entrances, 0.3s to 0.7s.
- Ambient glows breathe slowly.
- Technical labels draw in with lines, scans, and small pulses.
- Avoid frantic glitch unless it is used for a tiny accent.

## Constraints

- Keep all clips voiceover-safe. No overlapping spoken dialogue.
- No gradient text.
- Avoid generic blue-purple AI gradients.
- Use the existing architecture assets for the technical explanation.
